Elden Ring

Elden Ring is an epic action RPG developed by FromSoftware and published by Bandai Namco. Explore the vast Lands Between, battle powerful foes, and uncover deep lore in a dark fantasy world co-created by Hidetaka Miyazaki and George R.R. Martin.

Minimum System Requirements

  • OS: Windows 10
  • Processor: Intel Core i5-8400 / AMD Ryzen 3 3300X
  • Memory: 12 GB RAM
  • Graphics: GTX 1060 / RX 580
  • Storage: 60 GB
